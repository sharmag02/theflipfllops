About Department

Since its inception in 1981, the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering has been committed to delivering quality education in the field. Over time, student intake has increased to meet growing demand and industry opportunities. The department continuously updates its curriculum to reflect the latest technological advancements and industry requirements. Equipped with modern laboratories, software tools like MATLAB and Xilinx, and audio-visual teaching aids, it provides a conducive learning environment. The faculty is well-qualified and actively engaged in research across various areas of Electronics and Communication Engineering, with robust postgraduate and Ph.D. programs.


Vision of Department

The Department endeavours for academic excellence in Electronics & Communication Engineering by imparting in depth knowledge to the students, facilitating research activities and cater to the ever-changing industrial demands, global and societal needs with leadership qualities.


Mission of Department

The Mission of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering is:
1. To be the epitome of academic rigour, flexible to accommodate every student and faculty for basic, current and future technologies in Electronics and Communication Engineering with professional ethics.
2. To develop an advanced research centre for local & global needs.
3. To mitigate the gap between academia, industry & societal needs through entrepreneurial and leadership promotion.

About The Flip Flops

The Flip Flops, a vibrant platform solely dedicated towards celebrating the dynamic range of achievements, innovations, and academic pursuits of the ECE department. We offer you insightful articles, in-depth interviews, and engaging content from the core of our department.
The Flip Flops aims to foster communication and collaboration within the department and the wider community all around the globe. Our plan of action includes keeping the students, faculty, alumni and the industry associates informed and connected. We also act as a medium to highlight the work of our faculty and students to the world outside.

What We Offer:
• Academic Highlights: Stay updated with our talented faculty and students' latest research, publications, and academic achievements.
• Innovative Projects: Discover groundbreaking projects and innovative solutions developed by our students and research teams.
• Industry Insights: Gain insights into the latest trends and advancements in the field of electronics and communication engineering.
• Event Coverage: Get detailed coverage of departmental events, seminars, workshops, and guest lectures.
• Alumni Spotlight: Celebrate the accomplishments and career milestones of our esteemed alumni.
• Student Corner: A special section dedicated to student articles, opinions, and creative contributions.

Our Team Vision and Structure

Our vision is to craft an engaging and insightful platform that serves as a bridge between students, faculty, alumni and the wider academic community. Through our newsletter, we aspire to:
• Showcase Excellence: Highlight the academic, research, and professional accomplishments of our faculty and students, bringing their hard work and achievements into the spotlight.
• Build Community: Foster a sense of unity and collaboration by sharing updates on departmental events, workshops, guest lectures, and interdisciplinary projects.
• Inform and Inspire: Provide valuable insights into student placements, internships, industry collaborations, and evolving trends in the field of electronics and communication engineering.
• Encourage Innovation: Celebrate cutting-edge research, innovations, and technological advancements that are shaping the future of our discipline.
• Celebrate Success: Feature personal and professional milestones, inspiring stories of perseverance, and the collective growth of our department.

Each edition of the newsletter will document the department’s progress, inspire continuous learning, and cultivate a culture of innovation, preparing us to lead in the ever-evolving world of electronics and communication engineering.

Our newsletter team is organized to ensure smooth collaboration and high-quality output. The structure includes:
1. Teacher Coordinator
The Teacher Coordinator provides guidance and ensures the newsletter aligns with the department’s vision, while acting as a bridge between the team and administration.
2. Student Coordinator
Responsible for managing the team's operations, the Student Coordinator oversees deadlines, facilitates collaboration, and ensures smooth progress in each edition.
3. Content Writing Lead
The Content Writing Lead manages the writing team, ensuring all articles are engaging, well-researched, and reflect the department’s voice.
4. Graphic Design Lead
The Graphic Design Lead supervises the visual design team, ensuring that layouts, graphics, and images enhance the newsletter’s appeal and clarity.
5. Content Writing Members
Content Writing Members produce articles on department activities, achievements, and news, delivering informative and engaging content.
6. Graphic Design Members
Graphic Design Members create visual content such as layouts and infographics, ensuring the newsletter is visually attractive and cohesive with the written content.

This team works together to produce an engaging and informative newsletter that highlights the achievements and activities of the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department.

Join Our Team

Our recruitment involves two rounds:
Round 1: Skill-Based Test
Content writers complete a writing test, while graphic designers submit a design task to assess creativity and technical skills.
Round 2: Personal Interview
Shortlisted candidates will attend a personal interview to evaluate motivation, teamwork, and passion for the newsletter.
This process helps us form a talented and dedicated team to deliver a high-quality newsletter.
